Upload photos & videos to the 2025 photobook

Use this secure drop to send your best shots straight to the squad library. The form creates a pre-signed upload link so files land in the right monthly folder with the right metadata.

Tip: Batch uploads by month so the galleries stay organized. Captions can be updated later in the monthly pages or in the CMS.

Upload your images

Select a month, choose files, and we will generate secure upload URLs. You will see confirmation links when each image is stored.

Up to 20 files per batch · JPEG, PNG, HEIC, WEBP, MP4, MOV, or WEBM · Max 100 MB each

    Guidelines & storage plan

    • Files are stored in our secure object bucket with month-based folders for quick publishing.
    • Uploads expire after 15 minutes if not completed—refresh the page if a link times out.
    • Keep individual files under 100 MB for fast sync and mobile-friendly galleries.
    • Need to send RAW footage or video? Drop it in the archival bucket instead and note it in the comments.
    • Add auth tokens via data-upload-token on the form or a <meta name="sc9-upload-token"> tag if the API requires them.
    • Questions? Ping the #sc9-photos channel or review the upload runbook.
