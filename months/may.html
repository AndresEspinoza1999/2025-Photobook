Chapter 05 · Sunlit Weekends

May in focus

Long days meant more time to explore. We biked new trails, turned picnic baskets into photo props, and stretched every sunset ride into a mobile studio session.

BloomFest brought the crowd, but the quiet mornings—coffee on the stoop, planning summer itineraries—felt just as special. May reminded us to savor both the spectacle and the in-between.

Notable events

  • Sunset ride along the river with synchronized drone footage.
  • Community picnic potluck complete with chalk art and lawn games.
  • Front-row access at the BloomFest music stage.
  • Sunday sunrise coffee crawl scouting rooftops for summer shoots.
