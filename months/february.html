Chapter 02 · Winter Warmth

February in focus

The cold never stood a chance. We turned every icy forecast into an excuse for bonfires, night market snacks, and a cabin retreat where the living room doubled as a recording booth.

Between midnight board games and sunrise snowshoe loops, we kept the cameras rolling and the kettle full. February reminded us that staying close is an intentional choice—and one steaming mug at a time, we made it happen.

Notable events

  • Cabin retreat in the Blue Ridge with a fireplace jam session that lasted until dawn.
  • Lunar New Year night market crawl where we tried every skewer we could find.
  • Open mic night debut of the improvised “SC9 anthem” (with surprise harmonies).
  • Late-night snowball fight that ended with handwarmers and a group meditation.

Playlist
