April

Rain-soaked city shoots, birthday confetti, and the first outdoor film screening of the year.

Chapter 04 · Spring Pulse

April in focus

April refused to give us predictable weather, so we leaned into it. Rain slicked the sidewalks into mirrors, and every umbrella became a makeshift studio prop as we chased reflections around downtown.

When the clouds finally broke, we strung lights in the backyard, stacked blankets, and premiered our first short film between birthday cake intermissions. It felt like a soft launch of summer.

Notable events

  • Moody rain shoot downtown with neon reflections and a portable speaker soundtrack.
  • Back-to-back birthday parties complete with confetti cannons and midnight cupcakes.
  • Outdoor film screening where neighbors stopped by with extra popcorn bowls.
  • First bike-to-brunch Sunday complete with disposable cameras in the baskets.
