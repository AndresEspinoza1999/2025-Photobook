Peak summer energy: beach bonfires, fireworks over the bay, and a spontaneous road trip south.

Chapter 07 · Peak Summer

July in focus

The crew practically lived outdoors. We chased sunrises, grilled on the beach, and loaded the cars with instant cameras before heading south for salty air and starry skies.

Every stop—from the pier fireworks to tide-pool explorations—felt like a postcard waiting to happen. July is our annual reminder that spontaneity is a skill we proudly practice.

Notable events

  • Fourth of July fireworks shoot from the pier.
  • Bonfire cookout with sparklers and acoustic sets.
  • Road trip to tide pools and cliffside overlooks.
  • Sunrise paddle boarding session before the crowds hit the beach.

Playlist
