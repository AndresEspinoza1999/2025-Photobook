March

Cherry blossoms, spring cleaning parties, and a pop-up gallery we built from scratch.

Chapter 03 · Bloom Mode

March in focus

Winter gave way to fresh air and citywide color. We traded gloves for film packs, and the first hint of blooms had us planning entire weekends around morning light and evening lantern walks.

When the pop-up gallery finally opened, we watched our January and February stories projected on brick walls while friends left sticky-note compliments. March convinced us we could pull off anything together.

Notable events

  • Blossom picnic with homemade matcha, sketching prompts, and drone flights.
  • Spring cleaning swap where props, books, and vintage jackets swapped owners.
  • Pop-up “gallery night” projecting our winter favorites onto warehouse walls.
  • First riverfront ride of the year with disposable cameras taped to the handlebars.
